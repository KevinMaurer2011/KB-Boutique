Sizing Guide

Find your perfect fit with our comprehensive sizing guide. We want you to feel confident and comfortable in every piece from K&B Boutique.

How to Take Your Measurements

For the most accurate measurements:

  • Use a soft measuring tape - Keep it snug but not tight against your body
  • Wear well-fitting undergarments - This ensures accurate measurements
  • Stand naturally - Don't suck in or push out, just stand in your natural posture
  • Have someone help - It's easier to get accurate measurements with assistance
  • Measure over thin clothing - Or directly on skin for the most accurate results

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, which is typically the narrowest part of your torso.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, usually about 7-9 inches below your waist.

Fit Guide

Fitted

Close to the body with minimal ease. Choose your exact size for a sleek, tailored look.

Relaxed

Comfortable fit with room to move. Consider sizing down if you prefer a closer fit.

Oversized

Intentionally loose and flowy. Size down 1-2 sizes for a more fitted oversized look.

General Sizing Tips:

  • Between sizes? Size up for comfort, size down for a more fitted look
  • Different body proportions? Choose based on your largest measurement
  • Stretch fabrics tend to be more forgiving and can accommodate slight size variations
  • Non-stretch fabrics require more precise sizing for the best fit
  • When in doubt, contact us! We're happy to help you choose the right size
